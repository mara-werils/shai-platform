What document formats does shai.pro support?

shai.pro works with formats such as DOCX, EXCEL, PPTX, IMAGE, PDF, TXT, MD, JSON, and HTML.

If your format is different, contact us for clarification.

What models does shai.pro support?

We support all popular LLM models, including:
GPT by OpenAI (GPT-3.5, GPT-4, GPT-4 Turbo, GPT-4o), Mistral, Groq, Gemini, LLaMA by Meta, and Claude by Anthropic.

Can shai.pro be integrated with our systems?

Yes, shai.pro supports integration with CRM, ERP, BI, and other document management systems.

The only requirement is API connectivity on your side.

Can we create our own AI assistant?

Yes, you can create your own AI assistant. We provide detailed guides, training materials, and technical support.

Can we customize responses to match our corporate tone?

Yes. Provide examples of your brand style, and we’ll configure shai.pro to generate responses that follow your corporate guidelines.

Can we use multiple AI assistants for different departments?

Yes, shai.pro supports creating dedicated assistants for each department or task. Each assistant can have its own knowledge base.

Can our whole team use a single assistant?

Yes, shai.pro supports multi-user mode. The entire team can work with the same assistant simultaneously.

How accurate are shai.pro’s answers?

Answer accuracy depends on the quality of your data, the AI training, and regular knowledge base updates. We test each model to ensure maximum relevance and precision.

Are there any limits on queries or users?

shai.pro scales with your needs and supports high volumes of users and queries. Any limits depend on your selected pricing plan.

How much does shai.pro cost?

How long does it take to set up shai.pro?

Setup takes between 1 and 3 weeks, depending on the amount of data and task complexity.
